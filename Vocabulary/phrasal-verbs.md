# 🔄 Phrasal Verbs by Topic

> Grouped by theme so they're easier to learn and remember in context.

---

## Work & Career

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **take on** | accept responsibility/work | *She took on too many projects at once.* |
| **carry out** | perform / execute a task | *We need to carry out further tests.* |
| **put off** | postpone | *Don't put off what you can do today.* |
| **follow up** | check progress on something | *I'll follow up on that email tomorrow.* |
| **set up** | establish / create | *He set up his own company at 25.* |
| **step down** | resign from a position | *The CEO stepped down after the scandal.* |
| **burn out** | exhaust from overwork | *She burned out after years of stress.* |

---

## Communication

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **bring up** | mention a topic | *He brought up the budget issue again.* |
| **point out** | draw attention to | *She pointed out a flaw in the argument.* |
| **talk over** | discuss | *Let's talk this over before deciding.* |
| **back up** | support a claim | *Can you back that up with evidence?* |
| **come across** | seem / be perceived as | *He comes across as very confident.* |
| **get across** | communicate successfully | *It's hard to get my point across in English.* |

---

## Change & Progress

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **come up with** | think of an idea | *She came up with a brilliant solution.* |
| **work out** | resolve / exercise | *Things will work out eventually.* |
| **build on** | use as a foundation | *We can build on last year's success.* |
| **give up** | stop trying | *Never give up on something you believe in.* |
| **move on** | continue after difficulty | *It's time to move on.* |
| **catch up** | reach the same level | *I need to catch up on the news.* |
| **keep up with** | stay at the same pace | *It's hard to keep up with new technology.* |

## B2 Tip: Separable vs Inseparable

- **Separable**: object can go in the middle → *turn it off / turn off the light*
- **Inseparable**: object always after → *look after the children* ✓ ~~look the children after~~ ✗
