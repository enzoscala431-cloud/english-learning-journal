## ⚠️ Active Mistakes

### Grammar

| # | Wrong | Correct | Rule | Date |
|---|-------|---------|------|------|
| 1 | *I have went* | I have **gone** | Past participle of "go" is *gone*, not *went* | — |
| 2 | *She suggested me to go* | She suggested **that I go** / She **told me** to go | *suggest* doesn't take object + infinitive | — |
| 3 | *I am agree* | I **agree** | "agree" is a verb, not an adjective | — |
| 4 | *Despite of the rain* | Despite the rain / **Because of** the rain | *despite* never takes "of" | — |
| 5 | *The informations* | **The information** (uncountable) | information has no plural | — |

### Vocabulary (wrong word choice)

| # | What I wrote/said | Better option | Why |
|---|-------------------|---------------|-----|
| 1 | *It was a very good talk* | It was an **insightful / compelling** talk | "good" is vague at B2+ |
| 2 | *He is a very big person* | He is **influential / prominent** | "big" in this sense sounds unnatural |
| 3 | *I made a research* | I **did / conducted** research | *research* is uncountable; collocation is *do/conduct* |
