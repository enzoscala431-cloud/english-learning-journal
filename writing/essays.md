# ✍️ Essay Writing Guide — B2

> Use this as a reference before writing any essay. Consistency beats perfection.

---

## Opinion Essay Structure

```
Paragraph 1 — Introduction
  • Hook (question, statistic, or bold statement)
  • Background context (1–2 sentences)
  • Thesis: your position, clearly stated

Paragraph 2 — Main argument (strongest)
  • Topic sentence
  • Supporting evidence / example
  • Explanation of why this supports your view

Paragraph 3 — Second argument
  • Topic sentence
  • Supporting evidence / example
  • Explanation

Paragraph 4 — Counterargument + Refutation
  • Acknowledge the opposing view
  • Refute it with evidence or reasoning

Paragraph 5 — Conclusion
  • Restate thesis (different words)
  • Summarise main points
  • Final thought / call to action
```

---

## Useful Linking Language

### Adding ideas
- Furthermore, ... / Moreover, ...
- In addition to this, ...
- Not only ... but also ...

### Contrasting
- However, ... / Nevertheless, ...
- Despite + noun/gerund, ...
- Although + clause, ...
- While it is true that ..., ...

### Cause and effect
- As a result, ... / Consequently, ...
- This leads to + noun
- ... which means that ...

### Giving examples
- For instance, ... / For example, ...
- This is evident in ...
- A case in point is ...

### Concluding
- In conclusion, ... / To sum up, ...
- All things considered, ...
- It is clear that ...

---

## Avoid These at B2+

| ✗ Weak | ✓ Stronger |
|--------|-----------|
| *I think that...* | *It could be argued that...* / *In my view,...* |
| *very good* | *particularly effective / highly beneficial* |
| *a lot of* | *a considerable number of / a significant amount of* |
| *bad effects* | *detrimental effects / adverse consequences* |
| *because of this* | *as a result of this / owing to this* |
| *In my opinion I think* | *In my opinion* (don't double up!) |

## Sample Essay Opener

**Topic:** "Technology has made human relationships less meaningful."

> The rapid advancement of digital technology has fundamentally altered the way people connect with one another. While proponents argue that social media and messaging apps have brought the world closer together, critics contend that the quality of human connection has deteriorated significantly. In my view, technology has not inherently weakened relationships, but the way we choose to use it certainly can.
